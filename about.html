About Me

Bio

I am a student interested in technology, engineering, and solving real-world problems through hands-on projects. For my STEM Capstone, I created a Smart Garden System that automates plant care using a soil moisture sensor, water pump, and grow lights.

This project helped me improve my skills in coding, building, wiring, problem-solving, and design. It also helped me understand how technology can support sustainability and plant growth in a practical way.
