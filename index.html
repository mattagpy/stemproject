This portfolio shows my STEM Capstone project, including the problem, research, design process, testing, results, and reflection.

Project Overview

My STEM Capstone project is a Smart Garden System made to help automate plant care. The system uses a soil moisture sensor to detect when the soil is dry, then turns on a water pump to water the plant when needed. It also includes grow lights to support plant growth.

This project connects technology, engineering, and sustainability by showing how automation can make gardening more efficient and reliable.

Problem

Many people forget to water plants or do not know how much water plants need. This can lead to unhealthy plants or overwatering.

Solution

My Smart Garden System helps solve this problem by automatically checking soil moisture and watering the plant only when the soil becomes too dry.

Goal

My goal was to design and build a working system that supports plant care, saves time, and shows how technology can be used in real-life situations.
