Project Details

Problem Statement

People often forget to water plants or do not know the right amount of water to give them. This can lead to dry soil, overwatering, and poor plant health.

Project Purpose

The purpose of my Smart Garden System is to automate watering and support plant care by using sensors and simple technology to respond when the plant needs water.

Research

I researched how soil moisture sensors work, how water pumps can be controlled, and how grow lights help support plant growth. I also looked at how technology can improve efficiency in gardening and sustainability.

Materials

Wooden Box Arduino Soil Moisture Sensor Water Pump Tubing LED Grow Light Wires Power Supply

Design Process

I planned the layout of the box, selected the components, and figured out how to place the electronics, plant area, lighting, and wiring in one system.

Testing

I tested the soil moisture sensor and water pump to make sure the system could detect dry soil and activate watering at the correct time.

Results

The system was able to read soil moisture levels and turn the pump on when the soil became dry, showing that the project worked as intended.

Reflection

This project taught me how to combine coding, electronics, engineering design, and testing into one complete system. If I continue improving it, I would make the design cleaner, improve the wiring, and add more features for better performance and easier use.
